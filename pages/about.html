Who We Are

Guppy One Stop has been saving precious parent energies one stroller at a time since 2022.

We're two moms who have a passion for creating unforgettable experiences for families on the beach. We've been together for years, and when we both became moms, we discovered a shared interest in helping families enjoy their time in the sun with their little ones. Living on the beautiful island of Boracay, we quickly realized that there was a need for a convenient and safe way for families to explore the island's stunning beaches with their young children. That's why we decided to buy our first stroller that can withstand sandy beaches and bumpy roads. Naturally, we haven't looked back since!

Our mission is simple: we want to make your beach vacation with your children stress-free, fun, and memorable. We believe that every family deserves to enjoy the sun, sand, and sea, without having to worry about how to transport their little ones around the beach. We love what we do, and we're passionate about making your family's beach vacation unforgettable. So if you're planning a trip to Boracay with your little ones, don't hesitate to get in touch with us. We'd be honored to help you create some amazing memories on the beach!
